Who We Are

LuxeRide is a premium car showroom providing luxury vehicles with trust and quality service.

LuxeRide was founded in 1998 in Mumbai by a group of passionate car enthusiasts who wanted to change the way India buys premium cars. We believe buying a car should be exciting, transparent, and easy — not stressful.

Over 25 years, we have sold more than 20,000 cars, built lasting relationships with our clients, and earned the trust of the world's biggest brands — Porsche, BMW, Ferrari, Lamborghini, Rolls-Royce, and more.

Today, LuxeRide operates 3 showrooms across Mumbai, serving customers from all walks of life. Our philosophy is simple: every customer deserves the best car, at the best price, with the best service
